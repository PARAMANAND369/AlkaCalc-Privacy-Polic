Privacy Policy for AlkaCalc

Last Updated: March 2024

Introduction

Welcome to AlkaCalc. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our calculator application.

Information We Don't Collect

AlkaCalc is a simple calculator application that:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not require internet access
  • Does not store any calculation history
  • Does not share any data with third parties

How We Handle Your Data

All calculations are performed locally on your device. We do not:

  • Store any calculation history
  • Track your usage
  • Access your device's storage
  • Connect to any external servers

Permissions

AlkaCalc does not require any special permissions to function. It operates entirely offline and does not access any system features beyond basic calculator functionality.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal information from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app's updates.
